![Shaderpack-platform downloader on Shaderpacks tab under Prism Launcher settings](/img/screenshots/InstanceShaderpackPlatformDark.png)

In this page, you can download shaderpacks from Modrinth and CurseForge.

On the left, you can choose the shaderpack provider.

Clicking "Select shaderpack for Download" selects the shaderpack to be downloaded.

Clicking "Filter options" you can choose how strict the filters will be.

Clicking "Cancel" returns to the previous page.

Clicking "OK" allows you to see the shaderpacks you have decided to download and to download them.
