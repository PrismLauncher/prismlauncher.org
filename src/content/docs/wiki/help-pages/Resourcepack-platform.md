In this page, you can download resourcepacks from Modrinth and CurseForge.

On the left, you can choose the resourcepack provider.

Clicking "Select resourcepack for Download" selects the resourcepack to be downloaded.

Clicking "Filter options" you can choose how strict the filters will be.

Clicking "Cancel" returns to the previous page.

Clicking "OK" allows you to see the resourcepacks you have decided to download and to download them.
